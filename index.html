My Story

I’m a Midwestern girl who made the leap to Utah when my husband took a new job in 2013. (He’s at MX and I hear you guys will be neighbors soon!)

I got started in social media around 2011 by building my own brand, Thrift Simply, on Facebook. I built a community of over 1,200 people where kids items were bought and sold in a garage sale style group - back before garage sale groups were a thing.

After moving to Provo, I worked with the children’s moccasin company, Freshly Picked. I managed Pinterest and Facebook through the national attention that their appearance on ABC’s Shark Tank brought. I averaged 1000 new followers each week on Pinterest alone. I loved putting my creative skills to work in the fast paced world of a startup company.

Most recently I’ve been at Silhouette America managing all of the social channels as well as the affiliate program. I work to boost Silhouette’s social platforms and have seen the results pay off as the numbers continually grow. I also work directly with bloggers, companies, and online influencers who are a part of our affiliate program. A large part of the job is staying in tune with the customer’s feedback and responding to customer’s appropriately while passing the feedback on to the team. I’ve become the go-to employee who sees the whole picture of our customer’s point of view. I’ve enjoyed the challenges of working with a software/electronics company as well as curating well-formed content showing off DIY/crafting projects.

A while back, my personal profile caught Pinterest’s eye and a community manager contacted me to become a Pinfluencer. I am now a part of the Pinfluencer community where we give product feedback and test beta functions.
